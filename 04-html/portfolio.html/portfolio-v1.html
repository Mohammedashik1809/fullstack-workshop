Project Section:


Smart Building Sites Using AI & RSSI


My project, “Smart Building Sites Using AI and RSSI to Ensure Proper Worker Attire and Safety Compliance” focuses on improving safety at construction sites by combining artificial intelligence with IoT technologies. The system uses an ESP32 microcontroller integrated with sensors such as heart rate, temperature, and fall detection sensors to continuously monitor workers’ health and physical conditions in real time. These sensors help detect abnormal situations like sudden falls, unusual body temperature, or irregular heart rate, ensuring early identification of potential risks.

In addition, the project uses the YOLOv8 deep learning model with computer vision to automatically detect whether workers are wearing mandatory personal protective equipment (PPE) such as helmets and safety vests. To enhance worker tracking and rescue operations, RSSI (Received Signal Strength Indicator) technology is used to estimate worker location within the site by analyzing BLE signal strength. A real-time monitoring dashboard displays worker health status, PPE compliance, and location details for site supervisors.

The system is designed to generate instant alerts when safety violations or health issues are detected. A GSM module is integrated to send SMS notifications to the site engineer during emergencies, enabling quick response and reducing accident impact. Overall, this project aims to create a smart, reliable, and efficient safety monitoring solution that minimizes workplace accidents and improves overall worker safety in construction environments.
